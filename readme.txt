== Description ==

With this plugin, Create, Share votes, Debates, polls of the **Pipzoo** website with your friends and your family.

Measure your charm with your close relations ! Defend your opinions, your tastes, your passions, 
your favorite marks, your ideas on so many subjects as you it wished !

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the Pipzoo plugin and get it working.

1. Upload **`wp_mpipzoo.php`** to the **`/wp-content/plugins/`** directory
1. Activate the plugin through the **'Plugins'** menu in WordPress
1. Write a new article and put **"[mpipzoo vote_id]"** code.
* Exemple for url [http://www.pipzoo.com/view.php?v=JHYLnNYazFU](http://www.pipzoo.com/view.php?v=JHYLnNYazFU "Pipzoo Example Page")
* code : **[mpipzoo JHYLnNYazFU]**
1. Enjoy :-)
